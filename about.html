Who We Are

The Keeper's House Chapel International (TKHCI) is a Holy Spirit-led, Bible-believing Church founded on the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. The ministry was established on 16th August 2015 in Madina, Accra, with a divine mandate to bring healing, transformation, and meaning to lives through the good news of Jesus Christ.

Over the years, the saving power of Jesus Christ has faithfully guided the Church, strengthening us through sound teaching, committed leadership, and a deep passion for soul winning. The Church is presided over by the Global Lead Pastor, Rev. Francis W. Aubyn, together with his Co-Pastor and wife, Rev. Yacoba Aubyn.

TKHCI currently operates nine branches within Ghana and abroad, in addition to its head office branches. Each branch serves as a centre of worship, fellowship, discipleship, and outreach, ensuring that the gospel reaches people from diverse backgrounds.

The Church is also deeply committed to raising the next generation through purposeful ministries, including a thriving Teens Chapel and the Ministerial Training School, which trains and prepares vessels for the work of ministry.

In living out our faith beyond the four walls of the Church, TKHCI remains intentional about community impact. In celebration of our 10th anniversary, the Church undertook significant community projects, including the construction of a storm drain for the Madina community, annual blood donation exercises, and the distribution of clothing to those in need.

Through worship, teaching, discipleship, and community service, TKHCI remains committed to being a Christ-centered, community-based Church, faithfully advancing the Kingdom of God.

Our Leadership

Meet the dedicated leaders guiding our church family with wisdom, compassion, and spiritual insight.

Rev. Francis W. Aubyn

Global Lead Pastor

Rev. Francis W. Aubyn is the visionary founder and Global Lead Pastor of The Keeper's House Chapel International. With over two decades of ministry experience, he carries a profound calling to raise believers who understand their divine purpose and walk in spiritual authority. His teaching emphasizes practical Christianity, kingdom principles, and intentional discipleship.

Rev. Yacoba Aubyn

Co-Pastor

Rev. Yacoba Aubyn serves faithfully alongside her husband as Co-Pastor. With a heart for women, families, and community outreach, she provides spiritual guidance, nurturing support, and practical wisdom to the church. Her ministry focuses on building strong families, emotional healing, and empowering women to fulfill their God-given potential.
